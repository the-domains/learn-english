English in Use - Black to the future

"Black To The Future", was a News of the World headline on a report that one billion people had switched off their lights to support the Earth Hour energy conservation project. The headline is a play on the title of the hit 1985 sci-fi film 'Back To The Future'.

English in Use - Coo it's a coup!

"That's Quite A Coo" was a used in The Daily Mirror for a picture story about a pigeon emerging from the window of a house with a bagel around its neck. They used the word 'coo' because it's the sound a pigeon makes.
